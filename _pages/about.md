I am an Associate Professor in Responsible Leadership at [Trinity Business School](https://www.tcd.ie/business/), Trinity College Dublin, and a Fellow of Trinity College Dublin (FTCD). I currently serve as Academic School Director of [The Lir Academy](https://www.thelir.ie/), Ireland's National Academy of Dramatic Art, and as Academic Director of the MSc Management at Trinity Business School.

Before joining Trinity, I held positions at the University of St.Gallen and the University of Konstanz, and I was an invited visiting scholar at INSEAD, the University of Michigan (Ross School of Business), the University of Groningen, and IESE Business School. I received my PhD in Business Administration (with distinction) from the University of St.Gallen.

Research
======

My research centres around the interplay between individual and collective agency, asking: How can organisations help connect (previously disconnected) individuals across different backgrounds, perspectives, locations, and identities? In particular, I study:

1. **Communication across boundaries** – how employees communicate across team and organisational boundaries,
2. **Diversity** – how organisations manage demographic diversity within and between departments, and
3. **Human–AI interaction** – how managers interact with AI to perform people-related tasks.

I am interested in multi-level issues among individuals, organisational structures, and society. My work has appeared in journals such as the *Journal of Management*, *Journal of Management Studies*, *Human Resource Management*, *Journal of Business Ethics*, and *Academy of Management Discoveries*, and has been cited in policy reports by the OECD, the International Labour Organization, the European Commission, and the European Parliament.

**Keywords:** Leadership, Teams, AI
